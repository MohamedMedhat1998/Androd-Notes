        A couple of notes. In the latest version of ButterKnife, 7.x.x. the words have changed. @InjectView has become @Bind, and you have to call ButterKnife.bind instead of .inject. There are maybe a couple of other changes. Upgrading to 7.x.x will break code written for previous versions. Also, it's important that the static call to ButterKnife.bind (or ButterKnife.inject) is made in OnCreate after the call to setContentView - otherwise you will get an NPE.

        But other than that, it's a handy tip and it certainly makes for much cleaner code.
